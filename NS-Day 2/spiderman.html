Creation and Development

Spider-Man was co-created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. The character first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. Lee and Ditko aimed to create a character that would be relatable to teenagers, with struggles such as school, relationships, and self-doubt...
